Selfcare Tips

Simple, evidence-backed tips you can try today.

Breathing Exercise

Try 4-4-4 breathing: inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 4s for 1-2 minutes.

Short Walk

Take a 10–15 minute walk outside to reset your mood.

Sleep Hygiene

Keep a consistent bedtime, reduce screens before bed and avoid heavy late food/drinks.

Reach Out

Call or message a trusted friend — social connection works.
